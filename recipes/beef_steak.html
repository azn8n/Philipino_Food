Beef steak

Description

Beef steak also known as Bistek

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of beef
  • 1/3 cup squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

    • Instructions

    Preparing Ingredients

    1. Mince garlic

    2. Slice onion

    3. Slice Beef in to 1/4 inch pieces


    Cooking the Beef Steak

    1. Add beef, lemon, water, and soy sauce to a pot and let it boil till beef is tender

    2. In a pan, heat the vegetable oil

    3. Once oil in pan is hot, add garlic

    4. When the garlic starts to turn golden brown, add in onions and saute

    5. Once the garlic and onion is sauted and the beef is tender, add the garlic and onion to the beef

    6. Mix beef, onion, and garlic for a minute then let it boil for another 2 minutes

    7. Serve
