Pancit

Description

A Filipino noodle dish that uses wheat, rice, or mung bean noodles with various meats and vegetables. A staple at the dinner table or at any Filipino gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound chicken brest
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 cups chicken brother
  • 2 cups snow peas
  • 3 green onions
  • 3 pieces dried shiitake mushrooms (optional)
  • 1/4 pound mung bean thread noodles

    • Instructions

    Preparing Ingredients

    Note: you can use any vegetables that you desire in this dish, the ingredients that are listed are just for reference

    1. Cut chicken breast into thin slices

    2. Cut onion into thin slices

    3. Cut green onion into 1 inch lengths

    4. Soak 1/4 pound mung bean thread noodles in water for 30 minutes and cut into 3 to 4 inch strands

    5. (Optional) Soak shiitake mushrooms in water for 30 minutes then slice into thin strips

    Cooking the Pancit

    1. In a skillet saute the onions in hot oil until they are soft and translucent

    2. Add the chicken and saute for 3 to 4 minutes until meat becomes slightly firm and white

    3. Season the chicken and onion with the salt, white pepper, and fish sauce

    4. Add chicken brother and bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 10 minutes

    5. Add vegetables; snow peas, green onions, and mushrooms and cook for 4-5 minutes

    6. Add noodles and cook for 5 minutes while stiring

    7. Serve
