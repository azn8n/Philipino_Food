Chicken Adobo

Description

A Popular Chicken Filipino dish recognized throughout the world. Adobo refers to the sauce which can be used with other types of meats. Chicken adobo is one of my favorite dishes I grew up eating and still is today.

Ingredients

  • 3 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 1/2 - 3 pounds of chicken
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup Philippine vinegar or distilled white vinegar

    • Instructions

    Preparing Ingredients

    1. Grate ginger

    2. Mince garlic

    3. Chicken:

  • If you use just chicken breasts, cut the chicken into chunks.
  • If you pre cut pieces of chicken with the bone, you do not need to cut


    • Cooking the Chicken Adobo

    1. Heat oil in a large pot

    2. Add garlic and ginger to pot and saute

    3. Add chicken pieces till light brown

    4. Add vinegar, salt, pepper, water, and bay leaves.

    5. Put cover on pot and let ingredients simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is tender

    6. After 30 minutes or when meat is tender, remove the cover and continue to simmer until the liqud is reduced by half

    7. Serve with rice
